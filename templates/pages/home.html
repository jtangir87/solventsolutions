Our Proprietary Product

We are excited to introduce a new highly effective product into the world of sanitizing and disinfecting. OxiThyme is a proprietary system formulated with an EPA registered Hydrogen Peroxide disinfectant developed with a surfactant, or surface-active agent, improving efficiency by lowering surface tension. The addition of a surfactant and the use of an Oxi-Thyme spraying system allows the droplets of disinfectant to disperse evenly and cover larger surface areas, creating a no-touch solution to sanitizing large areas quickly and efficiently leaving no toxic residue.

OxyThyme is applied utilizing a sprayer-misting system, which uses an HVLP (higher volume - lower pressure) to aerosolize and propel the OxiThyme Disinfectant. This application process allows the droplets of disinfectant to disperse more evenly and cover larger surface areas, creating a no-touch solution to sanitizing large areas quickly and efficiently.

Services

Daily Sanitizer

Applied via a dispensing mister sprayed across any surface intended to be sanitized, and dries quickly.

Hydating Hand Sanitzer

Proprietary hands free dispensing station that is much gentler on skin because it is not alcohol or ethanol based, rather Hydrogen Peroxide based.

90 Day Sanitation

More aggressive sanitizing agent which again is applied through a spray process. It does require a short dry time, and is targeted to remain active for up to 90 days, however this time period may be shortened in high traffic areas.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike many competing products that require surfaces to be pre-wiped, then wipe on the product and return to wipe the surface dry again, OxiThyme can be applied utilizing this no-touch method and ensuring more surface areas are treated. OxiThyme dries in minutes, reducing down time needed to sanitize; more importantly, reducing down time for clients and customers.
